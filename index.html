About ITE

The Institute of Technical Education (ITE) in Singapore was established on 1 April 1992 as a post-secondary institution. An education from ITE provides graduates with good job and career advancement opportunities, as well as opportunities to go for further education and training.

Adopting a "One ITE System, Three Colleges" education model, ITE is committed to providing high-quality, market-responsive learning for the global economy. It offers a wide range of courses in fields such as Applied Health Sciences, Business & Services, Design & Media, Electronics & Info-Comm Technology, Engineering, and Hospitality.
